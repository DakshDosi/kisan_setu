What is Kisan Setu?

               

                    Kisan Setu is a revolutionary platform that connects farmers with cutting-edge agricultural technology and knowledge. Our AI-powered assistant provides personalized guidance in Hindi, helping farmers make informed decisions about crop management, weather patterns, and sustainable farming practices. Through intelligent conversation and data-driven insights, we're building a bridge between traditional farming wisdom and modern technology.
